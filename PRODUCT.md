# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Touhou fans reading the Secret Sealing Club booklet stories, usually while listening to the matching album on desktop or mobile.

## Product Purpose
Present the nine Secret Sealing Club albums as a small fan reading site where each album has its own page, each track can anchor a story passage, and the reader can switch between Chinese and Japanese.

## Brand Personality
Quiet, nocturnal, curious, scholarly, nostalgic.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, developer tools, to-B landing pages, loud glassmorphism, cluttered content portals, and copy that sounds like project tracking.

## Design Principles
- Keep one clear reading path per album.
- Let music and text feel synchronized.
- Keep entry points few and obvious.
- Use typography and spacing to carry calm.
- Let the cosmic atmosphere support the reading, not compete with it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Keyboard-friendly, mobile-friendly, reduced-motion aware, readable at WCAG AA, with clear language switching and touch targets that are easy to hit.
